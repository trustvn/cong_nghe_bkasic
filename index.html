About us

BKASIC Technology

BKASIC Technology is an integrated circuit design company founded in June 2017 by a group of executives and semiconductor experts from US and Vietnam.

BKASIC's vision is to be a leading high-speed IC design company in Vietnam. In particular, BKASIC is now focusing on the design of low power ultra-high speed optical driver and TIA integrated circuits for AOCs complying with USB and HDMI standards.
